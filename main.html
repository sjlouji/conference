About

About the Institution

The Loyola-ICAM (Institute Catholique d’Arts et Metiers) College of Engineering and Technology (LICET) is a Christian minority institution, founded in 2010 by the Jesuit Chennai Mission to form competent engineers with social responsibility. The Loyola College Society with its rich experience in Higher Education administers LICET in collaboration with ICAM group Lille, France. It is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. LICET offers a collective enterprise contributing to the formation of engineers who would promote growth in individuals and in society.

About the Department of IT

The primary objective of the Department of Information Technology at LICET is to prepare our students to apply their technological skills to comprehend and analyze complex problems to design and implement the feasible solutions. Graduates acquire the desire for lifelong learning and the ability to work in multi-disciplinary teams in meeting the global challenges. Graduates exhibit professional ethics, skills for management and responsibility towards societal needs. With rapidly evolving technology and the continuous need for innovation the department has always produced quality professionals.

About ICCIS

The Second International Conference on Computing and Information Systems (ICCIS ’19) focuses on the computing, information technologies, and information systems. It aims to provide a platform for sharing insights, experiences and interaction on various facets of evolving Technologies and patterns related to Information Technology. The main objective of the conference is to help students, research scholar, industry experts to share ideas and technology implementation. The conference encourages students, researchers and industrialists come with innovative ideas on latest technologies. ICCIS ’19 promotes technologists interaction and prolonged learning.

Paper Submission Guidelines

* The paper submitted to this conference must be original and must not be under consideration for publication elsewhere
* The paper should be written in clear, concise and grammatically correct English
* The paper length should be between 8 to 10 pages and formatted in Microsoft Word (.docx/.doc) with double-column format
* Unformatted papers and papers beyond the page limit may not be sent for review process
* The list of authors provided at submission time is final
* Electronic submission of paper (in MS-Word.doc, .docx) must be mailed to iccis.it@gmail.com
* For further information, click Author Guidelines

Review Process

* Papers will be checked for plagiarism subjected to Double-blind review.
* Selection criteria include originality of ideas, novelty, correctness, clarity and significance of results and quality of presentation.

Publication

* At least one author for each accepted paper must register for the conference for the submission to be included in the journal.
* Accepted and registered conference papers will be published in a UGC approved journal or a Scopus-indexed journals (with an additional cost).

  o International Journal of Engineering and Technology(UAE), ISSN: 2227524X – Scopus-indexed
  o International Journal of Applied Engineering Research (IJAER), ISSN: 09734562 – UGC Approved
